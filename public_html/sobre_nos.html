SOBRE A EXTREME COMPUTERS

(unofficial text/only for tests)Singularity Computers is an Australian based company and was officially established by Daniel Cannon in 2007. Our main focus is high end custom built computers, cases and components and we specialize in water-cooling and customization. We create unique designs based on client requests and work closely with our clients to create their dreams. We have a growing range of computer components. We provide many customization services including, case modifications, custom paint, air brushing, plating & anodizing etc. We take pride in the quality of our work; it is our number one priority. We put great importance on sharing information, which is why the Singularity Computers YouTube Channel is the centre of our company. You will find everything you need to know about what we do from the hundreds of hours of video on the channel.
